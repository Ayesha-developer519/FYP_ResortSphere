About this place

In a beautifully furnished apartment near the heart of Islamabad, settle into a spacious and elegantly designed retreat with stunning views of the Margalla Hills. This luxury apartment offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and traditional Pakistani hospitality.

The apartment features three spacious bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, a comfortable living area, and a private balcony. The master bedroom has a king-size bed with premium linens and an attached bathroom. Each room is air-conditioned and has high-speed WiFi throughout.

Whether you are here for business or leisure, you will find everything you need to make your stay comfortable and memorable. Nearby attractions include Faisal Mosque, Daman-e-Koh viewpoint, and the famous Centaurus Mall — all within a 15-minute drive.
